Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how Rentrovio uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Enabling essential website functionality
  • Analyzing how our website is used
  • Personalizing content and advertisements
  • Improving our services and user experience

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in to your account
  • Security: Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Load Balancing: Distribute traffic across our servers
  • Session Management: Maintain your session state

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Google Analytics: Track website usage and performance
  • Heatmap Tools: Understand user behavior patterns
  • Performance Monitoring: Identify and fix technical issues
  • A/B Testing: Test different versions of our website

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences:

  • Language Settings: Remember your preferred language
  • Theme Preferences: Save your display preferences
  • Form Data: Remember information you've entered
  • Notification Settings: Manage your communication preferences

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used for advertising and marketing purposes (with your consent):

  • Advertising Networks: Show relevant advertisements
  • Social Media: Enable social sharing features
  • Retargeting: Show ads based on your interests
  • Campaign Tracking: Measure marketing effectiveness

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

Analytics Services

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis
  • Hotjar: User behavior analytics
  • Mixpanel: Product analytics and insights

Marketing Services

  • Google Ads: Advertising and remarketing
  • Facebook Pixel: Social media advertising
  • LinkedIn Insight: Professional network advertising

Support Services

  • Intercom: Customer support chat
  • Zendesk: Help desk functionality
  • Calendly: Meeting scheduling

4. Cookie Duration

Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used for:

  • Maintaining your login session
  • Shopping cart functionality
  • Form data preservation
  • Security verification

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a specified period or until you delete them:

  • Short-term (1-30 days): User preferences and settings
  • Medium-term (1-12 months): Analytics and performance data
  • Long-term (1-2 years): Marketing and advertising cookies

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Learn more about each cookie category

Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of specific tracking services:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Hotjar Opt-out
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-out

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

Essential Cookies

Disabling these cookies may result in:

  • Inability to log in to your account
  • Loss of security features
  • Website functionality issues
  • Poor user experience

Non-Essential Cookies

Disabling these cookies may result in:

  • Less personalized experience
  • Repeated cookie consent requests
  • Less relevant advertisements
  • Limited analytics insights

7. Mobile Applications

Our mobile applications may use similar technologies:

  • Device Identifiers: Unique identifiers for analytics
  • Local Storage: Store app preferences and data
  • Push Notifications: Send relevant updates
  • Analytics SDKs: Track app usage and performance

You can manage these through your device settings or app preferences.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in our cookie usage
  • New features or services
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Industry best practices

We will notify you of significant changes through our website or email.

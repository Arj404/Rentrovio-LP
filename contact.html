Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

When will Rentrovio be available?

We're currently in beta development and plan to launch in early 2026. Join our waitlist to be notified when we're ready!

How much will Rentrovio cost?

Our pricing is $11.99/month for landlords with the first month free. Tenants and caretakers use the platform at no cost.

Do you offer integrations with other tools?

Yes! We're building integrations with popular accounting software, payment processors, and property management tools.

Is my data secure with Rentrovio?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and follow industry best practices for data security and privacy protection.

Can I manage multiple properties?

Yes! Our platform is designed to handle unlimited properties, from single units to large portfolios.

Do you provide customer support?

We offer comprehensive support including onboarding, training, and ongoing assistance to ensure your success.
